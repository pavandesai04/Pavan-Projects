What is SRE?

Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) is a discipline that incorporates aspects of software engineering and applies them to infrastructure and operations problems.

About SRE Team

Our SRE team is a group of skilled engineers dedicated to ensuring the reliability, performance, and scalability of our systems. We are passionate about automation, monitoring, and incident response.
